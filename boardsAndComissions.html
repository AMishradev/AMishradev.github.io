Boards and Commisions of Torrance

The City of Torrance currently has 13 commissions, boards, and councils that are advisory to the City Council. Specific information about commission meetings is included on each agenda cover. Call the department liaison for additional assistance. Commissioner appointments are made by the City Council as a whole from a list of certified applicants at a public meeting in June. Terms are for four years with a two-term limit.
