Heidi Ann Ashcraft, CouncilMember At-Large

Heidi Ann Ashcraft was appointed to City Council on May 12, 2020. She was first appointed to City Council on December 17, 2013 to fill the vacancy of Cliff Numark; and then was elected to retain her seat on June 3, 2014. She currently serves as Chair of the Community Planning and Design Committee, Chair of the Armed Forces Day Committee, and as a member of the Employee Relations and Public Safety Committee, Transportation Committee, Federal Legislative Advocacy Committee, She has previously served as chair of the Employee Relations and Public Safety Committee, and Transportation Committee; and as a member of the Strategic Plan Committee and the Transportation Committee, and as a member of the Special Standing State Legislative Advocacy Committee and the Special Standing Armed Forces Day Committee. She has served as the Alternate Voting Delegate for the League of California Cities - Los Angeles County Division, as well as for the Southern California Association of Governments (SCAG), the South Bay Cities Council of Governments (SBCCOG) and served 9 years on the Southern California Regional Occupational Center Board of Education. She also served as the alternate voting delegate for the Sanitation District of Los Angeles County. Ashcraft's involvement in the community includes being a founding member on the Torrance Human Relations Committee & Visions of Unity Art Competition, former Chairwoman of the Torrance-South Bay YMCA Board of Managers. She is currently a member of the Torrance Cultural Arts Advisory Board, the Torrance Historical Society, Old Torrance Neighborhood Association, Torrance Sister City Association, Friends of the Madrona Marsh and the Embroidery Guild of America. Heidi Ann Ashcraft also was elected to Torrance Unified School District Board of Education and served three terms. Heidi has four children, 3 grandchildren and another due in October, and is married to husband Dan, who currently serves as a Torrance Police Department Chaplain. Since 1986, she and Dan have owned Ashcraft Design, a product design consultancy in Torrance. Heidi Ann serves as the Chief Financial Officer.
