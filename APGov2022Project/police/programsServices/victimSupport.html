Victim Support

The Torrance Victim Support Program was created to ensure that support services are accessible to crime victims. The Victim Support Program is available to provide helpful and compassionate support to victims of crime and increase their participation in the Criminal Justice System. The Program provides such services as crisis intervention, counseling, resource and referrals and court support. Listed below are some of the services designed to assist the crime victims in the City of Torrance.

VINE Program

Vine is a free and anonymous telephone service that provides victims of crime two important features: information and notification. VINE monitors the custody status of offenders in the LA County Jail and receives updated information every 15 minutes via an interface with the LA County Jail booking system. Information is available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year and the service is available in English and Spanish.

In LA County, VINE can be reached at (877) VINE 4 LA (8463452) or online at http://www.vinelink.com/.
